An Image Gallery app is a web application that allows users to upload, organize, and view images in a visually appealing and user-friendly manner. It provides features such as image uploading, categorization, searching, and viewing. With the increasing popularity of digital photography and the proliferation of smartphones equipped with high-resolution cameras, image gallery applications have become an essential part of our lives. These applications provide a visually appealing and intuitive interface where users can store, sort, and categorize their photos for easy access and sharing.
